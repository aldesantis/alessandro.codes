title: Brands need to activate their purpose
---
Purpose is not irrelevant for brands, but it can’t just be lip service.

Purpose is only a meaningful strategic lever if a brand manages to act it out and activate it in a way that resonates with the consumer. When that happens, you [[turn-your-brand-into-social-capital|Turn your brand into social capital]].

If you don’t live your purpose, or if you live it in a way that doesn’t resonate, then it’s useless.
