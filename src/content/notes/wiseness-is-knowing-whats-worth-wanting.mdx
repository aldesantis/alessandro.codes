title: Wisdom is knowing what’s worth wanting
---
Getting what you want is a sign of intelligence.

Knowing what’s worth wanting is a sign of wisdom.

This is a useful general life principle, but it also applies to certain fragments of culture. For example, see how [[the-new-luxury-is-about-taste-not-wealth|the new luxury is about taste, not wealth]].
