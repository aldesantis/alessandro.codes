You cannot change a system until you understand the way it operates and the mechanisms it enacts in an attempt to [[systems-strive-for-self-preservation|preserve itself]]. In other words, before you break the rules, you need to go through the process of [[learning-is-a-four-phase-process|learning why they exist]].
