- Efficiency = moving fast
- Efficacy = moving in the right direction

Whenever we concern ourselves more with whether we can than with whether we should, we are prioritizing efficiency over efficacy. Generative AI is a prime example.

Efficiency is an endless race: you move faster and faster, but towards what?

However, at the same time, [[ai-moves-validation-downstream|AI moves validation downstream]], so efficiency sometimes also begets effectiveness.
