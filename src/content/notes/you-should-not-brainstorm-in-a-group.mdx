title: You should not brainstorm in a group
updatedAt: 2025-06-21T07:18:11.000Z
contentType: notes
---
Brainstorming in a group leads to converging prematurely.

Instead, people should brainstorm on their own (diverge) and then meet in a group to discuss their ideas (converge).
