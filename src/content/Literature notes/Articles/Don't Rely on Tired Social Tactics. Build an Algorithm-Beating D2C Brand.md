- The once-linear purchase funnel has [collapsed](https://www.adweek.com/performance-marketing/your-organization-needs-an-updated-revenue-model/), making consistent and compelling brand stories matter more than ever. Instead of seeing a direct equation in which Brand + Ads = Customers, brands today need to think like a media company that creates engaging, useful content that speaks to audiences in meaningful ways.
