title: Knowledge management

For someone who is interested in a million different subjects, having a good understanding of how knowledge is created and spreads is critical.

A good starting point is understanding that there are two types of knowledge, [[tacit-knowledge-is-hard-to-capture|tacit]] and [[explicit-knowledge-can-be-captured|explicit]], and that [[learning-is-a-four-phase-process|learning is a four-phase process]].
