At its core, marketing is the art and craft of [[marketing-must-build-availability|building mental and physical availability]] for a brand through advertising and distribution, which in turn enables brands to [[brands-serve-two-purposes|serve their functional and cultural purpose]].

These are some of the reads that have had the most influence on my opinions about marketing, so I would encourage you to start from here:

- [[the-new-strategic-brand-management|The New Strategic Brand Management]]
- [[how-brands-grow|How Brands Grow]]
- [[marketers-are-from-mars-consumers-are-from-new-jersey|Marketers Are From Mars, Consumers Are From New Jersey]]
