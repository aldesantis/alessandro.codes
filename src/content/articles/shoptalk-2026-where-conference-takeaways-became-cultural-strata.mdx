## Summary

Shoptalk 2026 showed how retail culture is changing through new tech and community trust. AI tools like ChatGPT help shoppers find what they want, while platforms like Reddit build honest, trusted spaces. Brands succeed by joining real communities and focusing on meaningful connections, not just ads.

## Highlights

- Reddit’s argument is that you can’t have artificial intelligence without actual intelligence — and that when people want information about a product or experience, they want to hear from people who have actually used that thing. In this case, the congregation is a subreddit. The ritual is the upvote.
