## Summary

GEO/AEO tools offer free crawlability and help with brand-level PR but mostly focus on broad storytelling, not product details. Many GEO/AEO tools are just rebranded SEO with issues like lack of actionability and unstable results. Some GEO/AEO content uses risky black hat SEO tactics, so brands should be cautious and focus on proven, ethical strategies.

## Highlights

- What GEO/AEO AI Visibility is giving you 100 prompts out of 100b long-tail permutations and showing you the results for that statistically irelevent sample. On top of that, it’s not actionable.
