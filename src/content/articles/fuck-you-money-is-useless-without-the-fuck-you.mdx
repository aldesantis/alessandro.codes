## Summary

Financial freedom doesn’t guarantee true independence; many people struggle with psychological barriers even after achieving it. Instead of just escaping a material prison, we need to confront our internal fears and find new purposes in life. Embracing this journey can lead to greater meaning and fulfillment beyond financial success.

## Highlights

- To me, this is one of the most important questions from the series: what’s the point of financial freedom if we’re still prisoners in our own minds?
