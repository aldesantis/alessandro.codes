## Highlights

- In psychology, when you attempt to control something that is ultimately uncontrollable, we call it “misapplied control.” As founders, it’s important to learn to recognize this pitfall and work with the underlying fear that drives it—the fear that [things might go terribly, horribly wrong](https://www.amazon.com/Things-Might-Terribly-Horribly-Wrong/dp/1572247118).
