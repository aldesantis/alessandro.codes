## Summary

Ritual -- which sells a suite of multivitamins -- is leveraging podcast ads to gain the attention of potential customers.

- Podcast listeners, on the other hand, are often more engaged with what they are hearing. Additionally, she said running an audio ad is much cheaper to produce and run than other marketing channels.
