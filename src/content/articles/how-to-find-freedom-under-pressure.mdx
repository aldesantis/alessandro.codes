## Summary

How to find freedom under pressure

## Highlights
- “Liberation,” viewed through a behavioral lens, is the ability to orient toward what’s appetitive (i.e., pleasant or meaningful), even in an environment full of aversive stimuli.
    - Tags: [[happiness]] [[career]] [[startups]] 
- It’s important not to do this as a way to try and [replace or get rid of what’s hard](https://every.to/no-small-plans/how-to-break-the-anxiety-fear-avoidance-cycle). That would only serve to keep you under aversive control. Instead, we want to make space to feel what’s difficult *and* open back up to what’s pleasant and life-giving in the world around us.
