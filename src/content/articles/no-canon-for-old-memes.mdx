## Summary

The Internet culture of the Aughts revolved around lo-fi images and videos. Will they be remembered by coming generations, and does this portend something about the future of culture?

## Highlights

- artistic _canons_ arise when gatekeepers designate certain materials from previous eras as “must-know,” and this triggers their repetition for new and old audiences
- Fangirl _output_ doesn’t exist in a format that can be canonized for broad audiences. Social media posts, memes, and wacky conspiracy theories are just less conducive for future enjoyment than artistic productions or clothing styles.
