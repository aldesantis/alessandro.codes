## Highlights
- artistic *canons* arise when gatekeepers designate certain materials from previous eras as “must-know,” and this triggers their repetition for new and old audiences
- Fangirl *output* doesn't exist in a format that can be canonized for broad audiences. Social media posts, memes, and wacky conspiracy theories are just less conducive for future enjoyment than artistic productions or clothing styles.
