## Summary

Why conflating your worth with work is a recipe for disappointment

## Highlights
- The research shows that when we develop multiple passions, interests, and identities, we don’t just become more well-rounded humans but—ironically—we get better at work too. People with greater self-complexity are [more resilient](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/3572732/) in the face of adversity, and [more creative](https://www.apa.org/monitor/2022/04/cover-science-creativity) problem solvers.
