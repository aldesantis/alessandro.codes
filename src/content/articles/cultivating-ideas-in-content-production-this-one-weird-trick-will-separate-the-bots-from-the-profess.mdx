## Summary

Content production should focus on the underlying ideas that connect a brand to its audience, rather than just generating content for immediate outcomes. Before creating content, teams must clarify their brand’s perspective and the unique value their ideas bring. By prioritizing thoughtful ideas, brands can foster deeper engagement and build long-term connections with their audience.

## Highlights

- _**The act of writing, of creating content, means presenting your business’ point of view on that topic.**_ It means that your business or brand chose to prioritize that content over something else on the to-do list, and the content’s reason to exist should be clear when production starts.
