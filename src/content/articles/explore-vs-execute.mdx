## Summary

Startups often struggle when transitioning from the “Explore” phase, where they discover what works, to the “Execute” phase, where they focus on scaling and operations. Founders who are overly confident in their initial success may fail to adapt to the new challenges, leading to chaos and potential disaster. This shift requires a change in mindset, processes, and team dynamics to ensure continued growth and stability.

## Highlights

- What Arrogant Founder doesn’t realize is that the needs of the company dramatically change after Product/Market Fit. Everyone and almost every thing at the company must change and adapt. Including the founder.
