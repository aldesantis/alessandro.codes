## Summary

As AI automates many tasks, humans will focus on five key jobs: trades, research, art, community, and stewardship. These roles rely on human judgment, creativity, connection, and responsibility that machines cannot replace. In the gentle singularity, meaningful work will be local, relational, and centered on what only people can do.

## Highlights

- **Technology follows a predictable path: first it’s a luxury for the rich, then it’s ubiquitous and cheap, then the human version becomes the luxury.** Handmade goods to mass production to artisanal craft. Live music to recorded music and back again. Humans doing what robots can becomes the ultimate luxury good.
