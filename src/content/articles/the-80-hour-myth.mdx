## Summary

The author argues that working long hours doesn’t guarantee success and suggests that true productivity comes from working less and focusing on quality. He emphasizes the importance of leisure and creativity, stating that our best ideas often emerge when we’re not actively working. By prioritizing a balanced lifestyle and understanding our energy cycles, we can achieve more in less time.

## Highlights

- The best way to create your ideal life is to live it, right now, but on a smaller scale.
