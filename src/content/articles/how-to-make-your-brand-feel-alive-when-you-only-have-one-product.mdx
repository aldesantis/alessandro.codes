## Summary

Lessons in world-building from the founders of Vintner’s Daughter and Athletic Greens.

## Highlights
- One good approach here is to make it feel — to yourself and your customers — that you’re elegantly solving a complex problem, not that you’re an incomplete version of your future self.
- When you don’t have a string of new products to build storylines around, tell the other parts of the story that make you *you*.
