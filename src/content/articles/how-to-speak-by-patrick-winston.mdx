## Summary

Patrick Winston’s video “How to Speak” offers valuable tips for effective public speaking, emphasizing the importance of clear communication. Key advice includes starting with a promise of what the audience will learn, using visuals to enhance engagement, and crafting a memorable ending. Winston also highlights the significance of preparing the setting and using tools like slides effectively to support your message.

## Highlights

- If you want to your ideas be remembered you’ve got to have “5 S”
  - Symbols associate with your ideas (visual perception is the best way to attract attention)
  - Slogan (describing your idea)
  - Surprise (common fallacy that is no longer true, for instance, just after you’ve told about it)
  - Salient Idea (not necessarily important but the one that sticks out)
  - Story (how you did it, how it works…)
