## Summary

The way creators are taught to pursue 1,000 true fans is broken

## Highlights

- The problem with niche strategy, in a nutshell, is that it’s a reliable destroyer of the human spirit. It’s built on the assumption that we can segment ourselves and turn one static aspect of the self into an economic engine that makes the rest of our life feel how we wish it’d feel.
    - Tags: [[niches]] [[strategy]]
- Niche strategy is an effective anesthetic for those parts of ourselves that squirm and scream when confronted with ambiguity and doubt, those parts that are too impatient and scared to play long games.
