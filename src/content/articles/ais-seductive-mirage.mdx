## Summary

AI systems seem advanced but are just tools that cannot make subjective decisions or meaning on their own. This misunderstanding can lead us to mistakenly rely on AI for tasks that require human judgment. We must recognize human meaning-making as essential for effective AI strategy and ensure that humans remain involved in decision-making processes.

## Highlights

- In thinking of AI systems as tools, the seductive mirage is that AI systems are (or can soon be) autonomous, self-governing systems that can make meaning like humans do. This is a mirage because [only humans can make meaning (for now)](https://uncertaintymindset.substack.com/p/ai-meaningmaking), and it is seductive because so much (money, reputation, etc) rides on believing that AI systems already can (or soon will) do anything humans can.
