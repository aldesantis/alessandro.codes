## Summary

Many retail brands focus too much on short-term results and neglect long-term vision. Without clear vision, brands struggle to connect with fast-changing culture and consumer needs. Companies need leaders who own and champion vision to guide their future direction.

## Highlights

- Most organizations have the systems to understand what happened, but [few can visualize and articulate](https://aws.amazon.com/data/cdo-report/) what’s next. Foresight requires interpretation and imagination, which are not easily automated and, frankly, require the time and space to wonder.
