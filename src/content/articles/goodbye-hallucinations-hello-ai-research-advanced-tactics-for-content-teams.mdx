## Summary

In the article, Tim Metz explains how content teams can enhance their research by using AI tools like ChatGPT Plus and Perplexity AI. These tools improve the quality and speed of research by summarizing information from various sources. Metz emphasizes the importance of verifying AI-generated content and treating it like a starting point for deeper investigation.
