## Summary

Meta Andromeda is Meta’s new retrieval engine that uses bigger models to pick the best ad candidates from millions.

It favors creative diversification, so provide many meaningful ad variations rather than over-tweaked duplicates.

Campaigns should be simpler while you spend more time crafting varied, effective creatives to drive real performance.

## Highlights

- Creative diversification shows up in a few key areas:
  - **Concepts and Angles** (Problem/solution, pain points, testimonials, product demos)
  - **Formats** (Short video, long video, static image, carousel)
  - **Personas** (Speaking to different customer types)
