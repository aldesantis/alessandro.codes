## Summary

With brands stuck in a rut, and the industry standing by, the time has come to chart a course that leads to new success.

## Highlights
- Clothing brands are among the companies that are most likely to fail in their early years. According to [Statistic Brain](https://www.statisticbrain.com/startup-failure-by-industry/), **just 47% of businesses in the retail industry still operate after the first 4 years of operation.**
