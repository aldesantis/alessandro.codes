## Summary

The Malleable Systems Collective aims to create software that is easy to customize, allowing users to modify their tools without needing advanced skills. They advocate for software that enables users to retain ownership of their creations and share them freely. The goal is to empower everyone to have better control and enjoyment in their computing experiences.

## Highlights

- We must empower people to tailor and customise their computing experience to support their personal workflows in ways which likely will never be prioritised or even imagined by the original application vendor.
