## Summary

Work that feels easy and natural can be more effective and sustainable than grind-style effort. When your real self and your work align, tasks flow and output rises. Help yourself or your team find that easy-fit work instead of measuring effort by hours.

## Highlights

- If you’re leading a team, the most effective way to get output is _not_ to mandate effort or hours. It’s to help people find work that feels _easy_ for them — not necessarily work they already know how to do, but work that feels obvious for them to get done by whatever means possible.
