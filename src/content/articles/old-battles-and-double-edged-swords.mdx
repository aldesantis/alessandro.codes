## Summary

Marketers at the Festival argued for simpler, broader strategies over hyper-segmentation and complex targeting. They urged focus on long-term mental availability—build memory and reach, not just short-term leads. Videos with human faces and tight positioning help cut through and align marketing with sales.

## Highlights

- And here’s a wakeup call: “British consumers wouldn’t care if 94% of brands disappeared.” In other words, nobody cares about your brand as much as you do. So, kill the complexity and focus on cut-through.
