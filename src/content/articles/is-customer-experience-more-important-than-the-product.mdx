## Summary

Customer experience (CX) is crucial for business growth, often more important than the product itself, as it impacts customer satisfaction and revenue. Companies struggle to align their various departments around a seamless customer experience, which can weaken their value proposition. Successful brands like Sephora show that a strong CX strategy integrates product functionality with customer support to enhance overall satisfaction.
