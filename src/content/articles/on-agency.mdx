## Summary

Agency means setting your own goals and believing problems can be solved. It requires learning, creativity, and not being afraid to try new ways. Life challenges helped the author become more agentic by forcing him to find practical solutions.

## Highlights

- I rarely feel blocked in the way I did when I was younger. But it is interesting that it was something I had to learn: that problems are solveable; that if I direct my attention to the problem and learn to understand it, and act on what I learn, the problem will, sooner or later, cave in.
- To be agentic, you have to really look at the problem and at the solution space and accept the responsibility of learning what is necessary to make the problem go away.
