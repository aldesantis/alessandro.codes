## Summary

Exploring the tension between being right and being useful

## Highlights

- There’s this fundamental tension as a consultant between being **right** and being **useful**. These things are not the same. There’s a question I keep in mind for every consulting project to remind me of this tension: _“What is the next most useful thing?”_
- Being strategic is simply about doing work in context.
