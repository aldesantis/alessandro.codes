## Summary

In this five-day email course, I’ll walk you through what procrastination actually is, why it’s got nothing to do with being lazy, the secret reason we become addicted to it, and—most importantly—how to stop.

## Highlights

- Procrastination is merely the avoidance of unpleasant emotions.
  Get comfortable with unpleasant emotions and procrastination takes care of itself.
