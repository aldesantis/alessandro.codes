## Summary

Digital gardening is an evolving collection of ideas organized in a non-linear way, contrasting with traditional blogs. It encourages personal exploration and knowledge accumulation, allowing for unique, creative expression online. This movement has gained traction due to communities embracing new tools and practices for sharing information.

## Highlights
- [](https://maggieappleton.com/garden-history/#footnote-7)Gardens are designed to evolve alongside your thoughts. When you first have an idea, it's fuzzy and unrefined. You might notice a pattern in your corner of the world, but need to collect evidence, consider counter-arguments, spot similar trends, and research who else has thunk such thoughts before you. In short, you need to do your homework and critically think about it over time.
