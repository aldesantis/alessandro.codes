## Summary

Hermann Hesse emphasizes the importance of finding joy in small moments rather than getting caught up in the busyness of life. He encourages people to appreciate simple pleasures, like nature and everyday experiences, to cultivate a sense of fulfillment. By practicing moderation and savoring these little joys, we can break free from the constant pressure of productivity.

## Highlights

- But the high value put upon every minute of time, the idea of hurry-hurry as the most important objective of living, is unquestionably the most dangerous enemy of joy.
