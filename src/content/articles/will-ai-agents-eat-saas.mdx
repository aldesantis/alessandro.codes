## Summary

AI agents are set to transform the Software as a Service (SaaS) landscape, potentially diminishing the value of traditional SaaS companies. Startups focused on AI-native solutions are rapidly achieving significant revenue milestones, highlighting the competitive pressure in the market. As AI technology matures, SaaS companies must adapt by improving user experiences and integrating digital labor to remain relevant and profitable.
