## Summary

In August 2022, the author recognized the potential of AI in content marketing and began experimenting with it in their agency. Initial efforts to use AI for efficiency led to disappointing results, as quality suffered and required extensive human editing. Now, they are integrating AI more thoughtfully into their processes, focusing on quality while leveraging AI’s capabilities for faster content production.

## Highlights

- We learned AI has no inherent value — you won’t see ROI just because you have AI. Like any tool, its worth comes from how skillfully you wield it and how well you integrate it into your existing processes.
