## Summary

The idea of two separate thinking systems, System 1 and System 2, is fictional. All thinking is really fast pattern matching based on experience. Experts solve problems by recognizing patterns, not by slow logical steps.

## Highlights

- The famous saying goes that “all models are wrong, but some are useful,” and I think that is true of these fictitious characters, as well.
