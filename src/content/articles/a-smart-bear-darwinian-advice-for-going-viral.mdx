## Summary

The author shares insights on how to make content go viral by comparing it to Darwinian evolution. To succeed, messages must encourage sharing, much like chain letters did in the past. Ultimately, the best strategy is to create genuinely valuable content that people want to share.

## Highlights

- So here’s the insight into building a viral message: **It’s not enough for the message to be interesting to your _direct_ followers (children), it needs to be something your followers would be interested in _repeating_ (grandchildren).**
