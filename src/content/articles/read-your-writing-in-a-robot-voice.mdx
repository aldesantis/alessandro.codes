## Summary

Today, we’ll cover an easy trick for how to edit your own writing and make sure your tone is coming across as you intended.

## Highlights

- When you read your writing in a robot voice, you might be surprised to discover that your note doesn’t sound as friendly as you thought. Some parts might even sound demanding, especially if you use a command sentence structure
