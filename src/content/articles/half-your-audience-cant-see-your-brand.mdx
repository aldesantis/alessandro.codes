## Summary

AI agents are increasingly answering customer questions for people, not brands. This means brands lose control of their story because agents synthesize data, not marketing. Companies must optimize both emotional branding for humans and accurate data for algorithms.

## Highlights

- But for AI agents, these elements are invisible. **Agents don’t “see” the brand**. They process performance data, review patterns, reliability metrics and factual consistency.
  So maybe branding doesn’t vanish. It bifurcates: **emotional for human touchpoints, data-driven and rational for agents**.
