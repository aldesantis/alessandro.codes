## Summary

Stitch Fix is focusing on its human stylists instead of just AI, highlighting the personal connections they build with clients. The company believes that combining human creativity with AI-driven recommendations enhances the shopping experience. As it adapts to competition and changes in the market, Stitch Fix aims to deepen relationships between stylists and clients through improved personalization and communication.
