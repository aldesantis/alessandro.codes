## Summary

The word “risk” is often used incorrectly to describe many uncertain situations that are not formally risky. Formal risk means knowing all possible outcomes, actions, and their probabilities, which is rare in real life. Using a formal risk mindset in other situations leads to bad decisions and outcomes.
