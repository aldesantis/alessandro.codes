## Highlights

- However, over the past year, I’ve found information paralysing rather than inspiring. The feeling of vertigo elicited when entering a bookshop, where the excitement derived from potential knowledge mingles with the overwhelming possibility of choice, is replicated 1000-fold each time I see a screen. There is too much information available and too many softly whispered ways to use it.
