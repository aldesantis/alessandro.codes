## Summary

The promise and pitfalls of zero-party data.

## Highlights

- Trying to pay individuals more money is a tricky model to follow and is likely to require a major industry shift. This means that instead of trying to pay users for their data, most companies would be better off providing [intrinsic value](https://www.adweek.com/brand-marketing/empowered-customers-are-driving-a-new-era-of-brand-meritocracy/), such as loyalty points or first-look previews of new product drops.
