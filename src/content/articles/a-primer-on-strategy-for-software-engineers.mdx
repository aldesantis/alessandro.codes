## Summary

Being strategic means creating a framework that guides decision-making and prioritizes actions aligned with goals. The article provides three useful frameworks for strategic thinking: Rumelt’s Kernel of Strategy, Playing to Win, and Three Horizons, which help break down complex challenges. Good strategy focuses on diagnosing issues, defining clear approaches, and balancing immediate needs with future opportunities.

## Highlights

- A good strategy helps shape our decisions. It isn’t just a plan, it’s a framework that opens some doors and closes others.
