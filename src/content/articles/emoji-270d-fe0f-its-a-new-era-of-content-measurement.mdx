## Summary

I’ve been thinking a lot about just how much content goals are changing––or need to change. So, this week, a bit of an essay about it, with no exact answers just yet.

## Highlights

- Zero-click content is the new landscape. Visibility is the new goal. Halo effects must be common knowledge internally. And attribution? Whew. For content, it might as well be going back to the Mad Men days and needing survey groups to truly understand.
