## Summary

The author discusses using walking as a tool and platform for creativity and productivity, sharing insights from a recent event and a long walk in Japan. By setting rules and constraints during his walks, he was able to enhance connection, creativity, and focus on his book projects. The author emphasizes the importance of being present and setting boundaries with technology to fully engage with the world around us.

## Highlights

- A smartphone excels, above all, at teleportation. It takes you from where you are, and places you elsewhere. If not physically, certainly mentally.
