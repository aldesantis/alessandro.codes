## Summary

Global ecommerce keeps growing fast, with AI-powered shopping driving big gains for Shopify and Walmart. Supply chains face new challenges from geopolitics and rising costs, while retailers adapt with AI and new strategies. Amazon expands as a universal shopping platform, and digital grocery sales show strong growth and innovation.

## Highlights

- This is a significant strategic pivot: Amazon is now positioning itself as a universal commerce layer, the agent that shops the whole web — while simultaneously blocking rival AI agents from accessing its own catalogue via robots.txt.
- Amazon blocking ChatGPT from crawling its products while simultaneously building its own agent to buy from other retailers is the marketplace equivalent of ‘what’s mine is mine, what’s yours is negotiable.’
