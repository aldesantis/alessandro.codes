## Summary

A new survey shows that U.S. consumers prioritize quality, fair pricing, and product reliability over a brand’s political or ethical values. While many consumers believe brands should express their values, most care more about delivering quality products. The research suggests that financial constraints and convenience often lead consumers to focus less on values-based shopping.

## Highlights

- “Despite the broad hype around boycotts, consumers do not seem to be increasingly voting with their wallets,” Thomas, who led the research, said in the release. “Consumers are feeling the pinch right now — money is tighter, time is tighter — and they will shop how, where, and as it best suits them.”
