Shielding Your Brain Against Time's Tide

## Highlights
- Recent research indicates that lactate produced during strenuous aerobic activity is not merely a waste product but a valuable energy source for the brain. It is transported to the brain via the bloodstream and is used by neurons as an energy substrate, particularly during periods of intense activity.
