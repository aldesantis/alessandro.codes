## Highlights
- You can think of Alexander Technique as coming in 5 steps, or 5 key ideas: 
  1. **Intention** 
  2. **Awareness** 
  3. **Pause** (take a moment instead of react) 
  4. **Non-doing** (actively don’t ‘do’; decline ‘doing’) 
  5. **Spontaneous, effortless action
