## Summary

AI chat platforms like ChatGPT will rely heavily on advertising to fund their growth. Ads must respect user privacy, be transparent, and genuinely help consumers make choices. This new “Sponsored Intelligence” will create a huge and powerful ad ecosystem shaping the future of AI and commerce.

## Highlights

- On the other side, if ads don’t change behaviors, they’re worthless. Google changes your results. Amazon changes your recommendations. Apple takes billions to make Google your default search. Every successful ads business in [history](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/oldest-ad-world-piece-content-marketing-alex-kirk/) has worked because it changed what consumers saw, thought, or did.
- AI capabilities will be everywhere and, inevitably, will be funded by advertising. The Sponsored Intelligence revolution is upon us, and it will be the defining concept in advertising for decades to come. The trillion dollar sentence - perhaps muttered seductively to you by your AI companion, perhaps embedded in a meta-review of product reviews by Rufus - will underpin an agentic economy that fuels unprecedented economic growth.
