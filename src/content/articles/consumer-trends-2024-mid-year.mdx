## Highlights

- The meaning of the word ‘inflation’ has changed. It used to mean rising prices; now it means high prices.
- Some ~60% of Gen. Z consumers indicated they were willing to keep buying from their favorite brands in a 2023 survey by McKinsey.
  For other, older generations, it was ~50%.
