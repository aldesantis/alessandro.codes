## Summary

Understand what filter bubbles are and how they affect your online experience. Discover ways to minimize their impact.

## Highlights

- By showing users similar products or services, the likelihood that they will make a purchase increases. This can have both positive and negative effects. On the one hand, this can lead to a better customer experience and customer satisfaction; on the other hand, users in a filter bubble can also miss out on important information or alternative offers.
