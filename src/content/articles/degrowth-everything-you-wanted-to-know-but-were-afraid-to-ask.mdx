## Summary

Degrowth is the idea of reducing production and consumption to address climate and ecological crises. It challenges the common belief that economic growth is essential for progress, suggesting that countries can thrive without it. The pursuit of degrowth aims to create a more sustainable economy while recognizing that growth can lead to environmental problems.
