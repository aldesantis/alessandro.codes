## Summary

In 2023, some brands’ executive boardrooms are still insulated from the chill of crypto winter, for better or worse. But the rising pressure of crypto skepticism has made it more urgent than ever for companies to figure out how to use blockchain technology to support their core offerings and customer base rather than simply dropping branded NFTs and hoping for the best.

## Highlights

- Skepticism around blockchain technology is rising — but the cold of crypto winter doesn’t appear to have frozen brands’ Web3 marketing budgets. Instead of pulling away from Web3, some marketers have instead expanded their definition of the concept, with an eye toward creating the [utility](https://digiday.com/marketing/utility-versus-speculation-why-brands-want-to-make-nfts-useful-rather-than-profitable-amid-the-crypto-downturn/) that was sorely lacking in many of the NFT drops of yesteryear.
