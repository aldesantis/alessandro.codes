## Summary

Big tech companies like Google use their money to make services around them cheap and interchangeable, so they stay on top. Google’s new Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) makes online shopping easy for AI agents, but it also lowers profits for sellers by making checkout simple and standard. This means the real power moves to who controls what people see and choose, like ads and recommendations.

## Highlights

- Seen this way, UCP is a move to turn the internet’s messy, human-shaped commerce layer into a clean, machine-shaped complement—so that advertising become even more central.
