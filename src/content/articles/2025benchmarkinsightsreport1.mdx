## Summary

The 2025 Digital Experience Benchmark report shows a decline in website traffic and rising costs per visit, highlighting the challenges businesses face in retaining customers. It emphasizes the importance of improving customer experience by reducing frustration and optimizing engagement to boost conversion rates. The report is based on data from over 6,000 websites and provides insights to help organizations enhance their digital strategies.

## Highlights

- Attracting visits was (again) more expensive than a year ago. Over the past two years, the cost of a visit increased +19% in total.
