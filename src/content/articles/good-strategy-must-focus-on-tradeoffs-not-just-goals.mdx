## Summary

Good strategy requires focusing on tradeoffs rather than just shared goals. When organizations only agree on goals without discussing necessary sacrifices, they often end up working against each other, causing inefficiency. To succeed, teams must explicitly agree on what they are willing to give up to achieve their objectives.

## Highlights

- Taking action with limited resources means making tradeoffs: Being ready to sacrifice some desirable outcomes to achieve outcomes that are even more valuable and desirable.
  **The key here is that tradeoffs must be desirable outcomes in themselves; this is what makes talking about and deciding on tradeoffs both difficult and essential.**
