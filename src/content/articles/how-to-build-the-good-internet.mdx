## Summary

The internet has become overly centralized and anxiety-inducing, leading many to seek refuge in smaller, more personal online spaces known as “The Cozy Web.” This movement emphasizes independent sites and communities that prioritize creativity and connection over corporate interests. To improve the internet, we should reclaim our ability to build and explore, fostering a more human-centered online experience.
