## Summary

If you’ve ever been excited about building a product in your teens or twenties, you’ve probably thought about an idea like one of these.

## Highlights
- And when this is the goal, the usual venture capital rules flip. It’s not about being *non-consensus right* (the VC holy grail). You can be consensus right with something that you believe in, even on a small scale, and still *succeed*.
- Critiques are valid, but they need context. Tarpit ideas can thrive by targeting niche markets and taking a lean, sustainable, and profitability-focused approach (among other strategies).
