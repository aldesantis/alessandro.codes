## Ingredients

- 8 leeks
- 8 medium potatoes
- 4 tbsp soy yogurt
- salt (as needed)
- pepper (as needed)
- olive oil (as needed)

## Method

1. Slice the leeks, peel and dice the potatoes, then saut√© everything in a pan for 5 minutes with a drizzle of olive oil.
2. Cover with water, put on a lid, and let simmer for 15 minutes.
3. Add the yogurt, adjust salt, and blend until smooth (with an immersion blender).
