## Ingredients

- Leeks (8)
- Medium potatoes (8)
- Soy yogurt (60 ml)
- Salt (as needed)
- Pepper (as needed)
- Olive oil (as needed)

## Steps

1. Slice the leeks, peel and dice the potatoes, then sauté everything in a pan for 5 minutes with a drizzle of olive oil.
2. Cover with water, put on a lid, and let simmer for 15 minutes.
3. Add the yogurt, adjust salt, and blend until smooth with an immersion blender.
