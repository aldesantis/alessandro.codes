## Ingredients

- Chicken wings (12, cut into sections)
- Barbecue sauce (300 ml)
- Extra-virgin olive oil (60 ml)
- Salt (15 ml)
- Chili flakes (1 pinch)

## Steps

1. In a bowl, toss the wings with half the BBQ sauce, the olive oil, salt, and chili (add more chili if you want it spicier).
2. Cover and marinate in the fridge for at least 2–3 hours, or overnight.
3. Preheat a fan oven to 200 °C.
4. Arrange the wings on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
5. Bake for about 30 minutes, turning and brushing with the remaining BBQ sauce every 10 minutes.
6. Serve hot.
