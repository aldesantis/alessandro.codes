### Ingredients (serves 4)

- 300 g cauliflower
- 150 g oat flour
- paprika
- turmeric
- black pepper

### Method

1. Make the dough:
    1. Clean the cauliflower, separate into florets, and steam for 15 minutes.
    2. Let cool slightly, then chop/blend into a purée.
    3. Mix in the oat flour and a pinch of salt. Rest for 10 minutes.
2. Cook the gnocchi:
    1. Compact the dough well.
    2. Dust a large cutting board with oat flour.
    3. Roll the dough into logs and cut into small pieces (about 1–2 cm).
    4. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and drop in the gnocchi.
    5. Cook for 1 minute.
    6. Drain and quickly toss in a pan, seasoning half with turmeric and half with smoked paprika.
