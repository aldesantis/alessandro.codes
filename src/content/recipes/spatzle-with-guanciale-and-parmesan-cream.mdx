### Ingredients

- spinach spätzle (amount as needed)
- Parmigiano Reggiano (amount as needed)
- guanciale (amount as needed)

### Method

1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
2. Cut the guanciale and cook until crispy.
3. Grate the Parmigiano.
4. When the water boils, reserve a ladle of cooking water.
5. Cook the spinach spätzle.
6. While the spätzle cook, mix grated Parmigiano with a bit of the cooking water to make a cream.
7. Drain the spätzle, mix with the Parmesan cream, and top with crispy guanciale.
