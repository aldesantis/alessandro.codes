## Steps

1. Prepare the sauce following the instructions for the [[bbq-sauce|BBQ Sauce]].
2. Prepare the dry rub: mix together all the spices (paprika, chilli powder, garlic powder, mustard powder, cumin, and salt).
3. Using a brush, spread the spice mix on both sides of the ribs.
4. Cover with cling film and let rest in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
5. Cover the ribs with aluminium foil and cook in a static oven preheated to 200°C for 1 hour and 30 minutes.
6. Remove the foil and continue cooking for another 30 minutes to let the liquids reduce. Every 5 minutes, take the tray out and brush the ribs with BBQ sauce until a caramelised, glossy surface forms.
7. Serve hot with the remaining BBQ sauce on the side.
