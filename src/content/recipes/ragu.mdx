## Ingredients

- Ground beef (250 g)
- Pork ribs (100 g)
- Sausage (1)
- Beef short rib (100 g)
- Lean beef (100 g)
- Ossobuco (1)
- Veal bones (3)
- Celery (1)
- Carrots (2)
- Onion (1)
- Triple-concentrated tomato paste (30 ml)
- Tomato passata (350 g)
- Salt (to taste)
- Pepper (to taste)
- Extra virgin olive oil (to taste)
- Red wine (½ glass)

## Steps

1. Chop the celery, carrot, and onion and sauté them in a separate pot with a bit of extra virgin olive oil and salt.
2. Brown the beef short rib and lean beef until golden, then transfer them to the pot where the ragù will cook.
3. Sizzle the sausage and pork ribs with a bit of cold water.
4. When everything is well roasted, deglaze with good red wine.
5. Add the tomato paste and the passata.
6. Add the bones, the ossobuco, and a ladle of water.
7. Cook for at least five hours over very low heat.
