## Ingredients

- Mezze maniche rigate (320 g)
- Canned tuna in olive oil, drained (250 g)
- Tomato passata (400 g)
- Garlic (1 clove)
- Fresh parsley (1 small bunch, to chop)
- Fresh chilli pepper (1)
- Extra virgin olive oil (40 g)
- Anchovy fillets in oil, drained (15 g)
- Salt (to taste)
- Pepper (to taste)

## Steps

1. Bring a large pot of water to the boil, salt it generously, and set it aside ready to cook the pasta.
2. In a wide pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat and let the whole, peeled garlic clove sizzle for a few seconds, then remove it.
3. Add the drained anchovy fillets to the pan and let them dissolve, adding a splash of pasta cooking water to help.
4. Stir in the fresh chilli pepper, sliced into strips (remove the seeds if you prefer a milder heat).
5. Drain the oil from the canned tuna, add the tuna to the pan and let it flavour for a minute or two, adding another splash of pasta cooking water.
6. Pour in the tomato passata and cook for about 10 minutes over medium heat.
7. Meanwhile, cook the mezze maniche in the boiling salted water until al dente, then drain them directly into the sauce.
8. Stir everything together for a moment, season with salt and pepper, and garnish with freshly chopped parsley to taste.
9. Serve immediately.
