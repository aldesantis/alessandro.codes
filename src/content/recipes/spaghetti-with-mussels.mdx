## Ingredients

- Mussels (500 g)
- Spaghetti (200 g, no. 5)
- Garlic cloves (2)
- Parsley (2 bunches)
- White wine (½ glass)
- Chili pepper (1, optional)
- Salt (as needed)
- Pepper (as needed)
- Canned peeled tomatoes (400 g, chopped)
- Olive oil (as needed)

## Steps

1. Scrub the mussel shells with a small knife (or another mussel).
2. Pull off the beard (the filament) by tugging outward.
3. In a tall pot add olive oil, half a chili, a bunch of parsley, and 1 smashed garlic clove.
4. Cook the mussels over high heat with a lid on until they open.
5. Separate the mussels from the cooking liquid.
6. Strain the liquid through a fine-mesh sieve or sterile gauze.
7. In a wide pan sauté 1 chopped garlic clove in olive oil.
8. Add 3–4 shelled mussels and sauté briefly.
9. Deglaze with white wine.
10. Add the tomatoes and the strained mussel liquid.
11. Add pepper and simmer covered for 15–20 minutes.
12. Shell the mussels.
13. Cook the spaghetti in lightly salted water until halfway done.
14. Transfer the pasta to the sauce and finish cooking, adding pasta water if needed.
15. In the last minute, add the shelled mussels.
16. Finish with black pepper and chopped parsley.
