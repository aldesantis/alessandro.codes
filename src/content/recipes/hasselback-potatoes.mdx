## Ingredients

- Potatoes (1500 g)
- Grana Padano DOP (150 g, to grate)
- Rosemary (1 sprig)
- Thyme (4 sprigs)
- Oregano (4 sprigs)
- Extra-virgin olive oil (50 ml)
- Garlic (1 clove)
- Fine salt (as needed)
- Black pepper (as needed)

## Steps

1. Wash the potatoes well under running water.
2. Dry them, then skewer each potato about 1 cm from the base.
3. Use a knife to slice the potato into thin cuts. The skewer helps you make even cuts without slicing all the way through.
4. Rinse the potatoes again to remove excess starch, then drain in a colander.
5. Finely chop the rosemary, thyme, oregano, and garlic.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and mix the herbs together.
7. In a bowl, combine the chopped herbs with the grated cheese.
8. Heat the oven to 200 °C.
9. Remove the skewers and fill the cuts with the herb-and-cheese mixture. Spread any extra mixture over the top as well.
10. Place the potatoes on a baking tray lined with parchment paper and drizzle with a little oil.
11. Drizzle a bit more oil over the potatoes.
12. Bake in a static oven at 200 °C for about 75 minutes (or 180 °C for 65 minutes in a fan oven).
13. Remove from the oven, let them cool slightly, and serve warm.
