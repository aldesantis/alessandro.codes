## Steps

1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil for the pasta.
2. Rinse the bream fillets.
3. Remove the skin and pick out any bones.
4. Cut into 1 cm cubes.
5. For option 1, slice the shallot thinly, dice the celery, then sauté with oil in a nonstick pan. For option 2, sauté the frozen soffritto mix with a drizzle of oil for 2–3 minutes.
6. Add the bream and chopped tomatoes.
7. Deglaze with white wine and let evaporate.
8. Add tomato passata.
9. Season with salt and pepper.
10. Cook for 3–4 minutes.
11. Drain the pasta and add directly to the pan.
12. Add a drizzle of oil and toss well.
13. Serve with chopped pistachios and finely sliced basil.

## Notes

- You can use frozen bream fillets.
- You can swap bream for sea bass, cod, or grouper.
- Can be made the day before.
- Great for special occasions (Christmas Eve, New Year’s, Easter).
- If using a frozen soffritto mix, avoid carrots (their sweetness can throw off the flavor).
