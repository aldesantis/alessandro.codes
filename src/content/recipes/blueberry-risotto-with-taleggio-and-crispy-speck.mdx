## Ingredients

- Carnaroli rice (320 g)
- Blueberries (320 g, 70 g small ones to freeze + 250 g for the purée)
- Taleggio DOP (150 g)
- Parmigiano Reggiano DOP (100 g)
- Butter (100 g)
- Speck Alto Adige IGP (4 slices)
- Shallot (1)
- Lemon thyme
- Extra-virgin olive oil
- Dry white wine
- Vegetable stock
- Salt (as needed)

## Steps

1. Pick about 70 g of small blueberries and freeze them on a tray.
2. Lay the speck slices on a small baking tray and bake at 120 °C, letting them dry and crisp up slowly.
3. Cook 250 g blueberries in a small pan with 20 g butter and a pinch of salt for 3–4 minutes.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Finely chop the shallot and gently sauté it in a small pan with a drizzle of oil.
6. Toast the rice dry in a pot, then deglaze with a glass of white wine.
7. Add the shallot and a ladle of stock. Cook for about 15 minutes, adding hot stock as needed.
8. Add the remaining butter, the Parmigiano, and the Taleggio.
9. Stir vigorously to emulsify, then let rest covered for 1 minute.
10. Stir in the blueberry purée.
11. Serve topped with the frozen blueberries, the crispy speck, and lemon thyme leaves.
