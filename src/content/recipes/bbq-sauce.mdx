## Steps

1. Finely chop the onion and garlic.
2. Sauté the onion and garlic in a small saucepan with a drizzle of oil, stirring frequently until golden.
3. Add the brown sugar, sweet paprika, and hot paprika, stirring continuously.
4. Pour in the Worcestershire sauce, apple cider vinegar, and mustard.
5. Add the black pepper, maple syrup, and finally the ketchup.
6. Season with salt without overdoing it and cook for about 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
7. Blend with a hand blender until smooth and uniform.
