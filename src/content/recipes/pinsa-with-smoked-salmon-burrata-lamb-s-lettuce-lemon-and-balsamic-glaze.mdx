## Steps

1. Heat the pinsa bases in a preheated oven at 220 °C for 5–6 minutes, until crisp outside and soft inside.
2. Meanwhile, drain the burrata and tear it into irregular pieces.
3. Wash and dry the lamb’s lettuce.
4. Make a quick emulsion with 30 ml olive oil, a few drops of lemon juice, a pinch of salt, and pepper.
5. Once the pinsa is out of the oven, let it cool for a couple of minutes so it doesn’t ruin the burrata.
6. Spread the burrata pieces, add the smoked salmon slices and the lamb’s lettuce.
7. Dress with the lemon-and-oil emulsion.
8. Grate a little lemon zest over the top for freshness.
9. Finish with thin drizzles of balsamic glaze.
