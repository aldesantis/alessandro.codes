## Ingredients

- 00 flour (500 g)
- Plain yogurt (250 g)
- Water (100 g)
- Fine salt (7 g)
- Fresh baker’s yeast (2 g)

## Steps

1. Put the flour in a bowl.
2. Add the yogurt and crumble in the yeast.
3. Pour in most of the water and mix with a wooden spoon to incorporate the yeast.
4. Add the salt and the remaining water.
5. Knead in the bowl, then transfer to a work surface.
6. Keep kneading until smooth and no longer sticky.
7. Return to the bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let rise for 4 hours at room temperature.
8. Transfer to a lightly floured surface and divide into 8 equal pieces.
9. For each piece, fold the edges toward the center, then shape into a tight ball.
10. Place the balls on a floured tray, cover, and let rise for 1 more hour away from drafts.
11. Lightly flour the work surface and roll each ball to about 1 cm thick, shaping round or oval.
12. Heat a nonstick pan until very hot.
13. Cook the naan for about 1 minute on the first side, until bubbles form and the bottom is browned.
14. Flip and cook for about 1 minute more.
15. Serve hot.

## Notes

- After cooking, brush with butter or ghee.
- Or serve with a simple oil brine: olive oil, salt, minced garlic, and cilantro (or parsley).
- Keeps for 1 day at room temperature.
- Can be frozen after cooking.
