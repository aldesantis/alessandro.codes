## Ingredients

For the salad:

- Cucumbers (400 g)
- Cherry tomatoes (200 g)
- Canned sweet corn (120 g, drained)
- Red onion (½)
For the yogurt dressing:
- 0% fat Greek yogurt (120 g)
- Extra virgin olive oil (15 ml)
- Fine salt (1 pinch)
- Fresh mint (5 leaves)

## Steps

1. Peel the cucumbers and slice them into rounds, thin or thick depending on your preference.
2. Wash the cherry tomatoes and cut them into quarters.
3. Slice the red onion thinly.
4. In a small bowl, mix together the Greek yogurt, olive oil, salt, and finely chopped mint leaves until smooth and creamy.
5. In a large bowl, combine the cucumbers, tomatoes, red onion, and drained corn.
6. Season with a pinch of salt and a drizzle of olive oil, and toss well.
7. Add the yogurt dressing and stir again to coat everything evenly.
8. Finish with a few extra fresh mint leaves.
9. Serve immediately, or refrigerate until ready to serve.

## Notes

- Substitute the Greek yogurt with any plain white yogurt.
- You can also dress this salad with feta cream for a bolder flavour, or with tzatziki for a classic Greek-style twist.
- Keeps well in the fridge for a few hours (great for a packed lunch).
