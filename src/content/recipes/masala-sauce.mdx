### Ingredients (serves 4)

- 250 g canned peeled tomatoes
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 1/2 golden onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 chili pepper
- fresh ginger (as needed)
- extra-virgin olive oil (as needed)
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tbsp mild curry powder
- salt (as needed)
- cilantro (as needed)
- lime juice (as needed)

### Method

1. Finely chop the onion, garlic, ginger, and chili. Sauté in a large pot with a little oil, adding cumin, paprika, curry, garam masala, and salt.
2. Cook for a few minutes, then add the peeled tomatoes and 1 glass of water.
3. Simmer, stirring often, and occasionally crush the tomatoes with a spoon.
4. Add the coconut milk and cook for about 5 minutes more. Finish with chopped cilantro and lime juice.

### Storage


Keeps for 4–5 days in the fridge in an airtight container.


### Notes


This recipe is free of gluten, soy, peanuts, tree nuts, and sesame.
