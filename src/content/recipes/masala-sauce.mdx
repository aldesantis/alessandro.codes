## Ingredients

- Canned peeled tomatoes (250 g)
- Coconut milk (200 ml)
- Golden onion (½)
- Garlic (1 clove)
- Chili pepper (1)
- Fresh ginger (as needed)
- Extra-virgin olive oil (as needed)
- Ground cumin (5 ml)
- Paprika (5 ml)
- Garam masala (5 ml)
- Mild curry powder (15 ml)
- Salt (as needed)
- Cilantro (as needed)
- Lime juice (as needed)

## Steps

1. Finely chop the onion, garlic, ginger, and chili.
2. Sauté in a large pot with a little oil, adding cumin, paprika, curry, garam masala, and salt.
3. Cook for a few minutes, then add the peeled tomatoes and 1 glass of water.
4. Simmer, stirring often, and occasionally crush the tomatoes with a spoon.
5. Add the coconut milk and cook for about 5 minutes more.
6. Finish with chopped cilantro and lime juice.

## Notes

- Keeps for 4–5 days in the fridge in an airtight container.
- This recipe is free of gluten, soy, peanuts, tree nuts, and sesame.
