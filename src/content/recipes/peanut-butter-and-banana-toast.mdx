## Ingredients

- 50 g whole wheat bread (1 slices)
- 1 tablespoon peanut butter
- 1 banana
- 20 g dark chocolate (85% cocoa)

## Method

1. Toast the bread.
2. Spread the peanut butter evenly over the toasted bread using a spoon.
3. Slice the banana and arrange the slices on top of the peanut butter.
4. Grate the 85% dark chocolate evenly over the toast slices.
5. Serve and enjoy while warm.
