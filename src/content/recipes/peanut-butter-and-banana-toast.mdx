## Ingredients

- Whole wheat bread (50 g, 1 slice)
- Peanut butter (15 ml)
- Banana (1)
- Dark chocolate 85% cocoa (20 g)

## Steps

1. Toast the bread.
2. Spread the peanut butter evenly over the toasted bread using a spoon.
3. Slice the banana and arrange the slices on top of the peanut butter.
4. Grate the 85% dark chocolate evenly over the toast.
5. Serve and enjoy while warm.
