## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 100 g sunflower seed oil
- 1 level teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon wasabi powder

## Method

1. To prepare the wasabi mayonnaise, place the egg yolks in the jug of an immersion blender.
2. Add the rice vinegar, wasabi powder, and salt.
3. Finally, pour in the sunflower seed oil, making sure it covers the other ingredients.
4. Place the immersion blender switched off at the bottom of the jug, directly over the yolks.
5. Turn it on at maximum speed and slowly lift it as the mixture thickens, until a stable emulsion forms.
