### Ingredients (serves 4)

- ingredients for [Two-tone cauliflower gnocchi](https://www.notion.so/156be6a92e838057924fd04766ccfc3b)
- ingredients for [Paprika kale chips](https://www.notion.so/2b8585d1ca384c0ebebdf58e636acfc2)
- ingredients for [Leek and potato soup](https://www.notion.so/156be6a92e83807b9c33c071a106d399)

### Method

1. Make the [Leek and potato soup](https://www.notion.so/156be6a92e83807b9c33c071a106d399).
2. Make the [Paprika kale chips](https://www.notion.so/2b8585d1ca384c0ebebdf58e636acfc2).
3. Make the [Two-tone cauliflower gnocchi](https://www.notion.so/156be6a92e838057924fd04766ccfc3b).
4. Plate like this:
    1. Pour the leek soup at the base of the plate.
    2. Add a pinch of pepper.
    3. Add the two-tone gnocchi.
    4. Finish with the kale chips.
