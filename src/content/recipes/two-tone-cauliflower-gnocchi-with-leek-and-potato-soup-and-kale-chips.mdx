## Steps

2. Make the [[paprika-kale-chips|Paprika kale chips]].
3. Make the [[two-tone-cauliflower-gnocchi|Two-tone cauliflower gnocchi]].
4. Pour the leek soup at the base of the plate.
5. Add a pinch of pepper.
6. Add the two-tone gnocchi.
7. Finish with the kale chips.
