## Method

1. Make the [[156be6a92e83807b9c33c071a106d399|Leek and potato soup]].
2. Make the [[2b8585d1ca384c0ebebdf58e636acfc2|Paprika kale chips]].
3. Make the [[156be6a92e838057924fd04766ccfc3b|Two-tone cauliflower gnocchi]].
4. Plate like this:
    1. Pour the leek soup at the base of the plate.
    2. Add a pinch of pepper.
    3. Add the two-tone gnocchi.
    4. Finish with the kale chips.
