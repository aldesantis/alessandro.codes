## Ingredients

- Digestive biscuits (270 g)
- Butter (120 g)
- Nutella (500 g)
- Philadelphia cream cheese (500 g)
- Chopped hazelnuts (30 g)

## Steps

1. Melt the butter in a small saucepan over very low heat, then let cool slightly.
2. Blend the biscuits in a food processor until finely crushed and transfer to a bowl.
3. Add the melted (slightly cooled) butter.
4. Mix well until you get a sandy mixture.
5. Butter a 24 cm springform pan and line it with baking paper.
6. Pour in the biscuit mixture and press firmly with a spoon to form the crust.
7. Chill in the fridge for 30 minutes (or in the freezer for about 10 minutes).
8. Put the cream cheese in a bowl.
9. Add 15 ml of Nutella.
10. Mix well with a spatula.
11. Add the remaining Nutella and keep mixing until smooth.
12. Take the pan out of the fridge and spread the Nutella cheesecake filling over the crust.
13. Smooth the top, cover with plastic wrap, and chill for at least 6 hours (overnight is best).
14. Transfer to a serving plate and decorate with chopped hazelnuts.
