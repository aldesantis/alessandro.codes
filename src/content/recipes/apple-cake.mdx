## Ingredients

For the batter:

- Golden apples (1 kg)
- Eggs (4)
- Granulated sugar (180 g)
- Lemon zest (1 lemon)
- All-purpose flour (240 g)
- Baking powder (16 g)
- Extra virgin olive oil (120 g)
- Fine salt (to taste)
- Ground cinnamon (to taste)
For decoration:
- Granulated sugar (30 g)
- Powdered sugar (5 g)

## Steps

1. Crack the eggs into a large bowl, add the sugar and beat with an electric mixer until the mixture is light, fluffy and pale.
2. Stir in the grated lemon zest, then pour in the extra virgin olive oil in a thin stream while continuing to mix.
3. Add a pinch of salt and the cinnamon.
4. Combine the flour and baking powder, then sift them into the batter little by little with the mixer running, until you get a smooth, uniform mixture.
5. Halve, peel and core all the apples. Cut half of the apples into cubes of about 2 cm, and slice the remaining half into strips about 7–8 mm thick.
6. Fold the apple cubes into the batter gently with a spatula until evenly distributed.
7. Line a 24 cm tin with baking paper, pour in the batter and smooth the surface.
8. Arrange the apple slices neatly on top, then sprinkle over the 30 g of granulated sugar.
9. Bake in a static oven preheated to 180°C, on the middle rack, for approximately 60 minutes. Check for doneness with a toothpick before removing from the oven.
10. Allow the cake to cool completely in the tin, then turn it out and finish with a light dusting of powdered sugar before serving.
