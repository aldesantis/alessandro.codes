## Steps

1. Cut the puff pastry into circles using a muffin tin as a size guide.
2. Lightly oil a muffin tin and place the pastry circles inside.
3. Mix the spinach with the ricotta.
4. Blend with an immersion blender.
5. Season with salt.
6. Spoon the cream onto each pastry circle.
7. Add a few pieces of sun-dried tomato on top of each quiche.
8. Bake at 190 °C (static oven) for 20 minutes, until golden and crisp.
9. Optional: add pine nuts or other seeds. If you want them toasted, add them before baking.

## Notes

- Alternative: you can bake them on an oiled baking tray if you don’t have a muffin tin.
- You can make them the same morning or the day before.
- To serve, reheat for a few minutes in a hot oven.
