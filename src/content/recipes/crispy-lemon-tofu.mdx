## Steps

1. Pat the tofu dry with paper towels, then cut into sticks about 1 cm thick.
2. Make a marinade with soy sauce, sesame oil, lemon juice, and a pinch of pepper.
3. Marinate the tofu sticks for at least 20–30 minutes.
4. In a bowl, mix breadcrumbs, cornflour, and lemon zest.
5. Drain the tofu sticks and coat in the breading, pressing so it sticks.
6. Pan-fry with a drizzle of oil until golden on all sides, or bake at 200 °C for 20 minutes, flipping halfway.
7. Serve immediately with a few drops of fresh lemon juice.

## Notes

- More marinating time means more flavour.
- Substitute the extra-virgin olive oil with neutral oil for extra crispiness.
- Cornflour can be substituted with cornstarch.
