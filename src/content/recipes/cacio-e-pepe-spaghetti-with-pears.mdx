## Ingredients

- Ingredients for [[spaghetti-cacio-e-pepe|Spaghetti cacio e pepe]]
- Pear (1)

## Steps

1. Peel the pear and cut it into small cubes (as if it were guanciale).
2. Add a drizzle of oil to a pan, heat over medium heat, and add the pear.
3. Add a little water, cover with a lid, and cook until tender.
4. Set the pear aside.
5. Make [[spaghetti-cacio-e-pepe|Spaghetti cacio e pepe]].
6. Plate the spaghetti and add 15–30 ml of pear on top.
