## Ingredients

- Corn (2 cans, 285 g each, rinsed)
- Black beans (2 cans, 425 g each, rinsed and drained)
- Red bell peppers (2, diced)
- Garlic cloves (2, minced)
- Chopped shallot (30 ml)
- Salt (10 ml)
- Cayenne pepper (1.25 ml)
- Sugar (30 ml)
- Extra-virgin olive oil (135 ml)
- Lime zest (5 ml)
- Lime juice (90 ml)
- Chopped fresh cilantro (120 ml)
- Avocados (2)

## Steps

1. Rinse and drain the black beans and corn.
2. In a large bowl, combine corn, beans, peppers, garlic, and shallot.
3. Add salt, cayenne, sugar, olive oil, lime zest, and lime juice.
4. Mix well and refrigerate for a few hours.
5. Before serving, add diced avocado.
6. Mix gently and adjust salt and lime if needed.

## Notes

- Great served with tortilla chips.
