## Ingredients

- Banana (1, 100–120 g)
- Banana whey protein (50 g)
- Peanut butter (20 g)
- Rolled oats (40 g)
- Cinnamon (30–45 ml)
- Milk (250–270 ml, dairy or plant-based)
- Ice cubes (4–5)

## Steps

1. Blend everything in a Nutribullet for 30–45 seconds.
