## Ingredients

- 1 banana (100–120 g)
- 50 g banana whey protein
- 20 g peanut butter
- 40 g rolled oats
- 2–3 tbsp cinnamon
- 250–270 ml milk (dairy or plant-based)
- 4–5 ice cubes

## Method

- Blend everything in a Nutribullet.
- Blend for 30–45 seconds.
