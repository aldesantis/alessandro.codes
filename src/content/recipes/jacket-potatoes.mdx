## Steps

1. Wash the potatoes under running water, scrubbing them well to remove any dirt.
2. Dry them with a clean cloth.
3. Wrap each potato carefully in aluminum foil and arrange them on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
4. Bake in a preheated static oven at 200 °C for about 1 hour and 30 minutes.
5. At the end of cooking, check they are done by piercing them with the tines of a fork.
6. Remove the potatoes from the oven and, taking care not to burn yourself, place them on a cutting board.
7. Cut them lengthwise and open them up using two forks.
8. Season as you prefer and serve immediately.
