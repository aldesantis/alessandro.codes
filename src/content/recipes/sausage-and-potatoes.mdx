## Ingredients

- Sausage (600 g)
- Potatoes (500 g)
- Extra-virgin olive oil (as needed)
- Salt (to taste)
- Black pepper (to taste)
- Rosemary (1 sprig)
- Garlic cloves (2)

## Steps

1. Peel the potatoes, rinse, pat dry, and cut into wedges.
2. Arrange on a baking tray with olive oil, chopped rosemary, garlic, salt, and pepper.
3. Cut the sausage into pieces about 5 cm long and add to the tray.
4. Bake at 180 °C for about 40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
5. For extra browning, turn on the grill for the last 5 minutes.
