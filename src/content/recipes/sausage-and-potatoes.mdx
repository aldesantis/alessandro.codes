### Ingredients (serves 4)

- 600 g sausage
- 500 g potatoes
- extra-virgin olive oil
- salt
- black pepper
- 1 sprig rosemary
- 2 garlic cloves

### Method

1. Peel the potatoes, rinse, pat dry, and cut into wedges. Arrange on a baking tray with olive oil, chopped rosemary, garlic, salt, and pepper.
2. Cut the sausage into pieces about 5 cm long and add to the tray.
3. Bake at 180 Â°C for about 40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. For extra browning, turn on the grill for the last 5 minutes.
