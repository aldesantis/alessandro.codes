### Ingredients

- ingredients for [Coleslaw](https://www.notion.so/2c9be6a92e8380a8a04fda3b3bc6f82d)
- ingredients for [Pulled mushroom](https://www.notion.so/2a5be6a92e8380768f96fa10b07f0c61)
- hamburger buns

### Method

1. Make the [Coleslaw](https://www.notion.so/2c9be6a92e8380a8a04fda3b3bc6f82d).
2. Make the [Pulled mushroom](https://www.notion.so/2a5be6a92e8380768f96fa10b07f0c61).
3. In a pan, melt some butter. When hot, toast the buns until crisp.
4. Assemble each bun with a layer of coleslaw and a layer of pulled mushroom.
