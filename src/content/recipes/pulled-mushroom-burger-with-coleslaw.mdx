## Steps

1. Make the [[coleslaw|Coleslaw]].
2. Make the [[pulled-mushroom|Pulled mushroom]].
3. In a pan, melt some butter. When hot, toast the buns until crisp.
4. Assemble each bun with a layer of coleslaw and a layer of pulled mushroom.
