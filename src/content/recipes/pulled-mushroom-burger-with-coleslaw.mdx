## Ingredients

- Ingredients for [[2c9be6a92e8380a8a04fda3b3bc6f82d|Coleslaw]]
- Ingredients for [[2a5be6a92e8380768f96fa10b07f0c61|Pulled mushroom]]
- Hamburger buns (2)

## Method

1. Make the [[2c9be6a92e8380a8a04fda3b3bc6f82d|Coleslaw]].
2. Make the [[2a5be6a92e8380768f96fa10b07f0c61|Pulled mushroom]].
3. In a pan, melt some butter. When hot, toast the buns until crisp.
4. Assemble each bun with a layer of coleslaw and a layer of pulled mushroom.
