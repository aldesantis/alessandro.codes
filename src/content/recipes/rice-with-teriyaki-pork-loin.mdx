## Steps

1. Cut the pork loin into thin strips.
2. Rinse the rice, cook it with a small amount of water until it is absorbed, fluff it, and set aside.
3. Cut the carrots and zucchini into matchsticks.
4. Heat a drizzle of sunflower oil and sauté 1 smashed garlic clove for 1-2 minutes.
5. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for 7-8 minutes, keeping them crisp.
6. Lightly salt the vegetables, then set aside.
7. Grate the ginger and the other garlic clove.
8. Mix the soy sauce, BBQ sauce, sugar, and water to make the sauce. It should taste sweet-salty, not too acidic.
9. In the same pan, add another drizzle of oil and stir-fry the pork strips for 4-5 minutes, until lightly browned.
10. Add the sauce and reduce for 2-3 minutes.
11. Add the rice to the pan with the meat, then add the vegetables.
12. Stir-fry over high heat for 1 minute, until everything is well coated.
13. Serve in bowls and top with sesame seeds.
