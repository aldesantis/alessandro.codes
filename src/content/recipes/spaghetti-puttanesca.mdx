## Ingredients

- Large square spaghetti (320 g)
- Canned peeled tomatoes (800 g)
- Anchovy fillets in oil (25 g)
- Salt-packed capers (10 g)
- Flat-leaf parsley (1 bunch)
- Gaeta olives (100 g)
- Garlic (3 cloves)
- Dried chili peppers (2)
- Extra virgin olive oil (30 g)
- Fine salt (to taste)

## Steps

1. Rinse the capers under running water to remove excess salt, then pat dry and roughly chop with a knife.
2. Pit the Gaeta olives and crush them with the flat of the blade.
3. Wash, dry, and finely chop the parsley.
4. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and salt it for the pasta.
5. In a wide pan, heat the olive oil with the whole peeled garlic cloves, the crushed dried chili, and the anchovies.
6. Add the drained capers and cook over medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring frequently, until the anchovies dissolve and the aromas are released.
7. Add the roughly crushed canned tomatoes, stir, and cook for another 10 minutes over medium heat.
8. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.
9. Remove the garlic cloves from the sauce and add the crushed olives.
10. Stir in the freshly chopped parsley.
11. Drain the pasta directly into the pan and toss for about 30 seconds to bring all the flavors together.
12. Serve immediately.
