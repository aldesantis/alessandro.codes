## Steps

1. Blend the base: put the cooked chickpeas, very ripe bananas, maple syrup, peanut butter, and vanilla extract in a blender and blend until smooth.
2. Combine the rest: pour the mixture into a bowl, add the almond flour, baking soda, sea salt, and chopped chocolate, and mix until everything is incorporated.
3. Shape the cookies: spoon the mixture onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, forming 6 cookies.
4. Bake: bake at 180 °C for about 25 minutes, or until cooked through.
5. Cool: let cool, then serve and enjoy.
