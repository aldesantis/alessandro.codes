## Steps

1. In a bowl, combine the melted dark chocolate and the melted butter. Whisk until smooth.
2. Add the sugar and mix well until fully dissolved.
3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition, until the batter is creamy and uniform.
4. Add the sifted flour and the vanilla extract. Mix until smooth and lump-free.
5. Grease the aluminum molds and dust them with cocoa powder.
6. Pour about 30–45 ml of batter into each mold and level the surface.
7. Dust the tops with a light layer of cocoa.
8. Bake in a preheated static oven at 180 °C for 12 minutes (or at 170 °C in a fan oven for the same time).
9. Immediately turn the cakes out onto plates and dust with powdered sugar.
10. Serve warm to keep the center soft and creamy.

## Notes

- Serve right after baking: once cooled, the center will set.
- You can prep them ahead and keep them unbaked in the fridge for a few hours, or freeze them for up to 1 month. Bake from frozen for 13–15 minutes.
- For a different flavor, replace vanilla with cinnamon, or add a pinch of chili for a more intense version.
