## Ingredients

- Fresh salmon fillet (250 g, previously frozen / sushi-grade)
- Extra virgin olive oil (to taste)
- Lemon juice (to taste)
- Chopped pistachios (to taste)
- Salt (to taste)
- Black pepper (to taste)

## Steps

1. Make sure the salmon fillet has been properly frozen for safety. If using fresh salmon, freeze it at −20 °C for at least 24 hours, then thaw it in the refrigerator.
2. Remove the skin from the salmon with a sharp knife.
3. Slice the salmon into thin slices, then cut the slices into small cubes, carefully removing any bones.
4. Transfer the diced salmon to a bowl.
5. Season with a pinch of salt, black pepper, lemon juice, chopped pistachios, and extra virgin olive oil.
6. Place a ring mold in the center of a serving plate.
7. Fill the mold with the salmon and pistachio mixture, pressing gently with a spoon to compact it.
8. Carefully remove the ring mold.
9. Serve immediately.
