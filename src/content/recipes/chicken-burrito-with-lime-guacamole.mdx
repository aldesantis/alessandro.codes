## Steps

1. Mash the avocado.
2. Add lime juice, salt, and pepper.
3. Finely chop the shallot, tomatoes, and chili.
4. Mix everything with the olive oil.
5. Cut the chicken into strips.
6. Marinate with oil, honey, cayenne, lime zest, and lime juice.
7. Rest in the fridge for 30 minutes.
8. Cook the marinated chicken in a pan for 5–6 minutes.
9. Warm the tortillas.
10. Add cheddar and let it melt.
11. Fill with lettuce, tomatoes, beans, and chicken.
12. Fold in the sides and roll up.
13. Top with cheddar strips.
14. Broil at 250 °C for 3 minutes.
15. Serve hot, with guacamole on the side.

## Notes

- Keeps in the fridge for 1 day.
- Pairs well with [[black-bean-salad|Black bean salad]].
