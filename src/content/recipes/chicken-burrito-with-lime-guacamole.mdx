### Ingredients (makes 4 burritos)

- 4 tortillas
- 400 g sliced chicken breast
- 80 g lettuce
- 150 g cooked black beans
- 200 g sliced cheddar
- 200 g tomatoes

#### For the marinade

- 30 g extra-virgin olive oil
- 10 g lime juice
- zest of 1 lime
- salt (as needed)
- 1 g cayenne pepper
- 20 g honey

#### For the guacamole

- 1 ripe avocado
- 10 g shallot
- 10 g fresh chili
- 150 g tomatoes
- 10 g lime juice
- 20 g extra-virgin olive oil
- salt and pepper (as needed)

### Method

1. Make the guacamole:
    - mash the avocado
    - add lime juice, salt, and pepper
    - finely chop shallot, tomatoes, and chili
    - mix everything with the olive oil
2. Marinate the chicken:
    - cut the chicken into strips
    - marinate with oil, honey, cayenne, lime zest and juice
    - rest in the fridge for 30 minutes
3. Assemble the burritos:
    - cook the marinated chicken in a pan for 5–6 minutes
    - warm the tortillas
    - add cheddar and let it melt
    - fill with lettuce, tomatoes, beans, and chicken
    - fold in the sides and roll up
    - top with cheddar strips
    - broil at 250 °C for 3 minutes
4. Serve hot, with guacamole on the side.

Keeps in the fridge for 1 day.
