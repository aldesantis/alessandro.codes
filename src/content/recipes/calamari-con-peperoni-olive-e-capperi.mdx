## Steps

1. Clean the squid: detach the tentacles from the bodies, remove the eyes and the central beak (between the tentacles), remove the innards and cartilage from the bodies, then peel them. Cut everything into rings, rinse under running water, and pat dry well with kitchen paper.
2. Prepare the bell peppers: remove the stem, halve the peppers, and remove the white membranes and seeds. Cut into thin strips, rinse, and pat dry with kitchen paper.
3. Heat the extra virgin olive oil in a pan and briefly brown the garlic clove.
4. Add the bell peppers and sauté for 4–5 minutes, stirring often with a wooden spoon.
5. Add the squid to the peppers, mix well, then add the sliced olives and the capers.
6. Stir everything and continue cooking for 15–20 minutes over medium-low heat, with the lid half-closed.
7. Adjust salt and pepper, sprinkle with chopped parsley, plate, and serve.
