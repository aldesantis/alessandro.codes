## Ingredients

- 60 ml ketchup
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 0.5 teaspoon dashi powder (optional)

## Method

1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl until the sugar and the dashi powder are completely dissolved.
