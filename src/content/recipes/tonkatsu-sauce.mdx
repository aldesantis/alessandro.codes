## Ingredients

- Ketchup (60 ml)
- Worcestershire sauce (30 ml)
- Oyster sauce (15 ml)
- Sugar (15 ml)
- Dashi powder (2.5 ml, optional)

## Steps

1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl until the sugar and the dashi powder are completely dissolved.
