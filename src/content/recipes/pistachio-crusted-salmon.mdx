### Ingredients (serves 2)

- 2 salmon fillets
- 50 g chopped pistachios
- 2 tsp mustard
- 2 tsp honey
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- extra-virgin olive oil (as needed)
- salt (as needed)
- white pepper (as needed)

### Method

1. Marinate the salmon in a baking dish with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
2. Cover and marinate in the fridge for about 1 hour (less if needed).
3. Spread mustard on each fillet, then spread honey.
4. Press the chopped pistachios onto the salmon.
5. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 °C for 12–15 minutes, depending on thickness.
