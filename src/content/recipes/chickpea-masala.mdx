### Ingredients (serves 4)

- 460 g canned chickpeas
- 1 batch of [[1c6be6a92e8380bfb271cc498d52d48e|Masala sauce]]
- cilantro (as needed)
- basmati rice (80 g)

### Method

1. Drain the chickpeas.
2. Make the [[1c6be6a92e8380bfb271cc498d52d48e|Masala sauce]].
3. Add the chickpeas to the masala (when you add the coconut milk) and simmer for about 5 minutes.
4. Serve with basmati rice and finish with more chopped cilantro.
