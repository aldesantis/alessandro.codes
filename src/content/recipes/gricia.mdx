### Ingredients (serves 2)

- ingredients for [Spaghetti cacio e pepe](https://www.notion.so/15cbe6a92e83803481f7c8fecfaa701e)
- guanciale (50 g)

### Method

- Cut the guanciale into strips.
- Brown over low heat until crispy.
- Set the guanciale aside.
- Make [Spaghetti cacio e pepe](https://www.notion.so/15cbe6a92e83803481f7c8fecfaa701e) in the guanciale pan.
- Top with the crispy guanciale.
