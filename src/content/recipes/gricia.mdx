### Ingredients (serves 2)

- ingredients for [[15cbe6a92e83803481f7c8fecfaa701e|Spaghetti cacio e pepe]]
- guanciale (50 g)

### Method

- Cut the guanciale into strips.
- Brown over low heat until crispy.
- Set the guanciale aside.
- Make [[15cbe6a92e83803481f7c8fecfaa701e|Spaghetti cacio e pepe]] in the guanciale pan.
- Top with the crispy guanciale.
