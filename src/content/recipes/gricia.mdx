## Ingredients

- Ingredients for [[spaghetti-cacio-e-pepe|Spaghetti cacio e pepe]]
- Guanciale (50 g)

## Steps

1. Cut the guanciale into strips.
2. Brown over low heat until crispy.
3. Set the guanciale aside.
4. Make [[spaghetti-cacio-e-pepe|Spaghetti cacio e pepe]] in the guanciale pan.
5. Top with the crispy guanciale.
