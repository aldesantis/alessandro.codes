## Highlights
- Marketing, distribution, merchandising, retail experience, merch, PR are not anymore separate from collections; they are part of the same stack, and custom-made for each. This requires creative collectives in place of siloed organizations, lateral collaboration instead of top-down management, and distributed instead of centralized creativity.
