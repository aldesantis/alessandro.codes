Hey, I'm Alessandro.

I am a software engineer based in Rome, Italy. I specialize in designing and implementing beautiful payment experiences and RESTful APIs, but I have done and do much more.

Currently, I am disrupting the industry of roadside assistance as the CTO of Batteries911.

This is some of the stuff I do.

I strongly believe OSS to be a gift, so I am committed to giving back to the community whenever I can. These are my most interesting projects.

Pragma

My most ambitious project, Pragma is a modular ecosystem for building RESTful APIs with Ruby. It provides everything you need to build your APIs in minutes.

pragmarb/pragma
Hertz

Hertz is a Ruby on Rails engine for sending in-app notifications. It supports different transports and works with both full-stack apps and APIs.

aldesantis/hertz
ARTIC

ARTIC is a Ruby gem for performing useful computations with time. It has been successfully used to build many types of scheduling applications.

aldesantis/artic
Bollettino

Bollettino is a gem for generating prefilled Italian postal payment slips, which customers can then use to easily pay your invoices.

interconn-isp/bollettino
Abaco

A by-product of Bollettino, Abaco is a handy Ruby utility for converting numbers into Italian words through a nice API resembling ActiveSupport.

interconn-isp/abaco

Finally, I am a writer.

Words are the most powerful weapon we have. I write both fiction and non-fiction, as well as the occasional essay. It's all yours to download.

Estasi e sofferenza

A short novel about the relationship between Eros and Thanatos, love and death, so apparently far apart and yet so hopelessly intertwined. Estasi e sofferenza is a story about the lengths we can go for those we want in our lives and how, very often, what is and what appears are two different things.

La Società Alternativa

We've all dreamt of a better world at least once, whether out of genuine love for our species or mere boredom – a world where man would be free from the burdens of ordinary life. What if we found that world is well within our reach? La Società Alternativa is a reminder of how easily our deepest dreams are molded.

L'ultimo guerriero

My very first (finished) production, this is a story of belonging, jealousy and motherhood. Sometimes, we can find ourselves in the least expected of places – but what happens when someone strips us of our new-found identity?

Specismo e benessere animale

What remains of my year-long dive into the world of specism and animal welfare, Specismo e benessere animale is a reflection on the state of animal testing, exploring the ideas of several different philosophers to reach what, I hope, is a clear conclusion.

Charlotte
WIP

Once again, a study of human relationships. We think love is above everything, but is it, really? By telling the most scandalous of affairs, Charlotte pushes the limits of our moral principles and forces us to reconsider our own concepts of right and wrong or, if you prefer, of love and deviation.

Riflessioni semiserie
WIP

A short collection of reflections on different topics: God, religion, the great paradoxes of philosophy and atheism and a few other matters. This is my brain dump, constantly updated with my latest findings and conclusions. It serves as a reminder of the volatility of thoughts and opinions.
