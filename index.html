Pragma

My most ambitious project, Pragma is a modular ecosystem for building RESTful APIs with Ruby. It provides everything you need to build your APIs in minutes.

pragmarb/pragma
Hertz

Hertz is a Ruby on Rails engine for sending and managing in-app notifications. It offers support for several different transports and works both with full-stack apps and APIs.

aldesantis/hertz
ARTIC

ARTIC is a Ruby gem for performing useful computations with time. Over the years, it has been successfully used to build many types of scheduling applications.

aldesantis/artic
Bollettino

Bollettino is a gem for generating prefilled Italian postal payment slips. These can then be sent to people by email or regular post to allow them to easily pay your invoices.

interconn-isp/bollettino
Abaco

A by-product of Bollettino, Abaco is a handy Ruby utility for converting numbers into Italian words through a nice API resembling ActiveSupport.

interconn-isp/abaco
