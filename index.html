Pragma

My most ambitious project, Pragma is a complete ecosystem for building RESTful APIs with Ruby (on Rails). Comprised of several different modules, Pragma provides everything you need to build your API in a matter of seconds.

pragmarb/pragma
Hertz

Hertz is a Ruby on Rails engine for sending and managing in-app notifications. It offers support for several different couriers, keeps track of deliveries and supports both full-stack apps and APIs out of the box, while also being highly customizable.

aldesantis/hertz
ARTIC

ARTIC is a Ruby gem for performing useful computations with time. Mainly, it can be used to determine when someone has a free slot in their calendar. ARTIC has been successfully used to build many types of scheduling applications.

aldesantis/artic
Bollettino

Bollettino is a gem for generating prefilled Italian postal payment slips. These can then be sent to people by email or regular post to allow them to easily pay your invoices.

interconn-isp/bollettino
Abaco

A by-product of Bollettino, Abaco is a handy Ruby utility for converting numbers into Italian words through a nice API resembling ActiveSupport.

interconn-isp/abaco
